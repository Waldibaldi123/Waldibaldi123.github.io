Risk Modelling

Can you identify a potental scammer company before it even exists? After multiple meetings with the CEO of ISHAP, a portal that helps Austrian building companies to safeley recruit workers, this was my mission for this internship. I did in-depth case studies on already identified scammers, brushed up on the current laws, and discussed important database aspects with the CTO. Then, I build an algorithm that scraps company data from the internet and, using the ORM pattern, efficiently inserted it into a relational database. Finally, I wrote python to automatically visualize company histories, making it easier to discover new patterns. My findings were presented to clients and the Austrian Ministry of Finance.

Rocket Communication Protocols

In a $200,000+ liquid-propellant rocket, communication within the components of the rocket and to the ouside world must be flawless. It needs to automatically fix errors produced by outside noice and have redundancy, since one wrong bit could mean the loss of the rocket. The rocket is 60 feet long, meaning that multiple embedded systems at the different parts of the rocket read their close-by sensors, communicate with each other and have to arrive at a certain output, like close valve-A or deploy parachute. Therefore, I had discussions and meetings with pretty much every department of RPL to understand the layout and functionalities of the rocket to arrive on the most optimal implementations. All work is thoroughly documented for the Base11 competition, but not available to the public.

C, Java, Electrial Engineering, Aerospace Engineering

Olive Moment Detection

As the final project of a datascience class, we used machine learning regression to more accurately determine the olive moment of comets in Comet Assays. To accomplish this, we collected a large number of data(images) that represent Comet assays. We wrote our own circle detection algorithm to identify the heads of comets. Following that, we created a Neural Network that uses the circle detection algorithm to identify heads and calculate the olive moments. In order to train the neural network, we compiled a set of images with corresponding olive moment calculations that we already had, and used that data to train the neural network. I specifically was responsible for the machine learning part of the project by utilizing Tensorflow. See Details for the jupyter notebook.

Python, Tensorflow, Data Analysis, Cell Biology
